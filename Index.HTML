Instant messaging background

Instant Messaging, which is commonly known as IM or IM’ing, is the process of exchanging of nearly real time text messages by means of a standalone application or embedded software. Compared to chatrooms which has many users interacting in multiple and overlapping conversations, IM periods usually happen between two or more users in a secure private back and forth way of communication (Nast, 15 June 2022). Students use instant messaging such as WhatsApp messenger, messenger for Facebook in order to communicate with teachers in terms of asking for assistance with homework and assignments. Instant messaging makes an impact on students on a national level because it enables communication not only locally but worldwide which means students from different nations or within the same nation can communicate together in real lifetime.
